Frequently Asked Questions

What do you hope to learn from this?
The Scikic allows us to:
  • Assess the potential that internet and social media data can provide to personalised medicine
  • Investigate how we might integrate data from multiple sources
  • Demonstrate to users how data may be integrated by 3rd parties
  • Develop user-centric data models in which data doesn't become accumulated into large single databases
  • Applying differential privacy to real data and inference
What if I want to delete my data?
Either type 'delete' into the scikic at any time, or contact us.
