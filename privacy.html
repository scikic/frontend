Scikic: Privacy Policy

The scikic uses data that you provide to produce insights that you might find entertaining or useful. It uses data you've given in answer to the questions it's asked, and from any services you've allowed it access to (facebook, spotify, etc).

What happens to the data is completely up to you, and you can change your mind in the future. There are three options:

Delete all data
After you've received your insights from the scikic, it will delete all the data it has received about you. You can control this by visiting your account page. To confirm your data has been deleted, or if the cookie associated with you has been lost please contact us.
Help train the Scikic
The default; Your data will be used to help improve or "train" the scikic and is then deleted.
Help personalised medicine
Not yet implemented, but in the future, your data will be thoroughly anonymised (using the differential privacy framework), and then made available for research use in the field of personalised medicine.

Cookies

The app uses a limited-lifetime cookie, which is essential for keeping track of who is who, and is therefore exempt from the EU cookie consent rule.
